# En direct / en différé

- status: active contrast
- updated: 2026-04-06
- sources: `sessions/2026-03-26_tcf_voc.md`, `06_vocabulaire/vocabulaire_master.json`

## Purpose

Canonical contrast page for one of the clearest temporal oppositions in media-related QCM.

## Core contrast
- `en direct` = broadcast at the same moment the event happens
- `en différé` = broadcast later, after recording

## Safe distinction
- live now -> `en direct`
- recorded first, broadcast later -> `en différé`

## Source-backed examples
- `Le match sera retransmis en direct ce soir.`
- `L'interview a été diffusée en différé.`

## Why this is a good QCM trap
The distractor is simple but fast:
- both options fit the media domain
- only one fits the time relationship between event and broadcast

## Reuse notes
- good for short VOC spot-checks
- useful as a model contrast page because the opposition is binary and concrete
