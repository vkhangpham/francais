## Purpose

Canonical contrast page for one of the recurring media vocabulary confusions in the current cycle.

## Core contrast
- `émission` = one specific show or broadcast
- `programme` = the full schedule or set of planned broadcasts; sometimes broader than a single `émission`

## Safe distinction
- if the sentence refers to one concrete show, prefer `émission`
- if the sentence refers to the schedule for the evening or the lineup, prefer `programme`

## Source-backed examples
- `J'écoute cette émission tous les matins.`
- `Le programme de ce soir commence à vingt heures et comprend trois émissions d'actualité.`

## Why this is a good QCM trap
Both words belong to the same broadcast domain, so they sound plausible.
The key is whether the sentence wants one unit or the overall set.
