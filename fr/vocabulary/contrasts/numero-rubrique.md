## Purpose

Canonical contrast page for another repeated media/presse confusion.

## Core contrast
- `numéro` = one particular issue or edition of a journal or magazine
- `rubrique` = one thematic section inside a journal or magazine

## Safe distinction
- if the sentence points to a whole issue released on a date, think `numéro`
- if the sentence points to culture, sport, politics, etc. inside the publication, think `rubrique`

## Source-backed examples
- `Le dernier numéro de cette revue est sorti ce matin.`
- `Je lis toujours la rubrique culture en premier.`

## Why this is a good QCM trap
The words both belong to the print-media universe, but they operate at different levels:
- `numéro` = whole edition
- `rubrique` = section inside the edition
