# Part 3 Opinion and Argument

## Purpose

Capture the logic of strong Part 3 answers so preparation does not depend on improvising from zero each time.

## Core challenge

Part 3 is harder than Parts 1 and 2 because success depends less on personal facts alone and more on:
- structuring an answer quickly
- taking and nuancing a position
- comparing options
- giving reasons and examples
- using connectors and rhetoric naturally
- sounding organized under time pressure

## Reusable goals
- answer the question clearly at the start
- organize the answer into 2 or 3 moves
- justify with one concrete reason or example
- add nuance, limit, or contrast when useful
- close cleanly instead of fading out

## Archetypes to build out in frameworks
- clear opinion
- balanced advantages/disadvantages
- nuanced agreement
- recommendation
- comparison and choice
- problem -> causes -> solution

## Link rule

Whenever a scenario produces a strong Part 3 move or structure, extract it into the relevant framework page instead of trapping it in one scenario.
