# Quirks and Style

## Confirmed preferences
- Scripts should be highly personal and easy to relate to.
- Generic textbook monologues are less useful than believable, specific wording.
- The final result should support solo out-loud rehearsal.

## Things to watch
- avoid wording that is grammatically correct but does not sound like Kyle
- avoid making the speech too literary or too long
- prefer reusable chunks that can migrate to other scenarios
